## What You Can Do

### For Hosts (organizers)

- **Create and manage events** — one-time or recurring. Add dates by duplicating.
- **Booth map designer** — drag-and-drop layout with background images, booth pricing, and vendor self-selection.
- **Vendor applications** — customizable forms with deadlines, auto-waitlist, and terms.
- **Vendor management** — approve/deny/waitlist, assign booths, request payments, add notes, DM vendors.
- **Digital Market Guide** — QR code for shoppers with interactive booth map, giveaways, and community signup.
- **Giveaways** — vendor-sponsored prizes, social follow requirements, automated winner selection.
- **Payments** — Stripe Connect for booth fees and custom payment requests.
- **Messaging** — direct messages with individual vendors, plus market-wide announcements.
- **Schedule page** — public page listing all your upcoming events.
- **Marketing** — sync vendors and shoppers to Mailchimp or Flodesk, export CSVs, loyalty program.
- **Print shop** — order signs, banners, labels, packaging, and more with customizable templates.

### For Vendors

- **Apply to markets** — one form, tracked across all events.
- **Find markets** — browse the [public market directory](https://seenmarkets.com/find-a-local-market) by state or location, explore in the app, or use host links.
- **Vendor portal** — dashboard with applications, payments, booth assignments, and messages.
- **Booth selection** — pick your own booth when the host enables it.
- **Direct messaging** — message hosts about specific events, plus market-wide group chat in the app.
- **Loyalty program** — earn rewards for repeat participation.
- **Become a host** — already a vendor? [Create an organization](/how-to/become-a-host) from your account to run your own events.
- **Mobile app** — manage everything from your phone.

### For Shoppers

- **Digital Market Guide** — interactive booth map, vendor directory, giveaways, community signup.
- **QR code access** — scan at the market entrance, no app download needed.
