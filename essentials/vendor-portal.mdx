## Overview

The Vendor Portal is where vendors manage everything about their market participation — applications, booth assignments, payments, messaging with hosts, loyalty rewards, and their business profile. It's available on the web at [seenmarkets.com](https://seenmarkets.com) and in the Seen Markets mobile app.

## Accessing the Portal

Vendors get access when they submit their first application or create an account. Sign in at [seenmarkets.com](https://seenmarkets.com) or the mobile app.

## Dashboard

The vendor dashboard shows:

- **Upcoming markets** — all events you've applied to, with status (pending, approved, waitlisted, denied).
- **Pending payments** — outstanding payment requests from hosts with a link to pay.
- **Market updates** — notifications from hosts (schedule changes, reminders, etc.).
- **Booth info** — your assigned booth or option to pick one (when self-selection is enabled).

## Managing Markets

### Application Status

Track every application:

- **Pending** — waiting for host review.
- **Approved** — you're in. Check for booth assignment and payments.
- **Waitlisted** — on the list; you'll be notified if a spot opens.
- **Denied** — not accepted for this event.

### Apply to More Dates

If a host runs recurring events, you'll see their other dates. Apply to additional dates with **one tap** — your business info is prefilled. Choose which dates you want to attend.

<Frame caption="See other dates to join">

### Update Your Application

Made a mistake or need to change something? Open the market and tap **Edit application** to update your submission (opens on the web).

### Cancel Participation

Changed your mind? Open the market details and use **Cancel participation** to withdraw. The host will be notified.

### Explore (app)

The **Explore** tab shows upcoming markets from hosts you've already worked with. An easy way to find more dates or new events from organizers you know.

### Find Markets (app)

Search for new markets by name, location, state, or "near me." Apply directly from the results.



### Explore (web)

At [seenmarkets.com/v/explore](https://seenmarkets.com/v/explore), browse markets from hosts you've worked with and apply to new dates.

### Find a Local Market (public directory)

Visit [seenmarkets.com/find-a-local-market](https://seenmarkets.com/find-a-local-market) to browse a public directory of all published markets on Seen Markets. You can search by name, browse by state, or use your location to find markets near you. No login required.

## Booth Selection

<Frame caption="Booth Selection">
  <img src="/images/vendor-pick.png" alt="Booth Selection" />
</Frame>

When a host enables booth self-selection:

1. You see the interactive booth map with available booths.
2. Select a booth. **Free booths** confirm immediately; **paid booths** go to Stripe checkout.
3. Reservations have a **time limit** — complete checkout before the hold expires.

If the host assigns your booth manually, you'll see your assignment in the market details and the booth map.

## Payments

### Payment Requests

When a host requests payment (e.g. booth fee):

- You receive an email with a secure Stripe checkout link.
- The request also appears on your dashboard and in the mobile app.
- Tap **Pay Now** to complete the payment.

### Payment History

View all past payments at [seenmarkets.com/v/payments](https://seenmarkets.com/v/payments) or in the mobile app under **Menu → Payment History**.

## Direct Messaging

Message hosts directly about specific events:

- **Web:** Open a market and use the messaging feature.
- **App:** Tap **Message organizer** from the market details, or go to the **Messages** tab for all conversations.

Messages are organized per-market so conversations stay in context.

## Loyalty Program

Some hosts run a **loyalty program** that rewards vendors for repeat participation:

- See your progress toward the next reward in the app's **Loyalty** tab or at [seenmarkets.com/v/loyalty-program](https://seenmarkets.com/v/loyalty-program).
- Rewards are typically discounts on booth fees based on number of markets attended.
- Each host's program is separate.

## Profile Management

Keep your vendor profile updated — it's used across all applications.

<Frame caption="Vendor profile">
  <img src="/images/vendor-profile.png" alt="vendor profile" />
</Frame>


### What you can edit

- **Business info** — name, description, product categories.
- **Logo** — upload your business logo.
- **Contact** — email, phone.
- **Address** — business address.
- **Social links** — Instagram, TikTok, X, Facebook, YouTube.
- **Payment methods** — Venmo, Cash App, PayPal usernames (for hosts to see).

<Note>
  Your profile info automatically fills future market applications, saving time
  and ensuring consistency.
</Note>

## Market Updates and Notifications

- Hosts can send market-wide updates (schedule changes, weather alerts, setup instructions).
- Updates appear on your dashboard and in the market details.
- You also receive an email notification.
- All updates are preserved for future reference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="How do I get portal access?">
    Your account is created when you submit your first application or sign up at
    seenmarkets.com. The mobile app uses the same account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="arrows-rotate" title="How do I update my profile?">
    On the web: go to your profile page. In the app: tap **Edit Profile** from
    the header. Changes apply to all future applications.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock-rotate-left" title="Can I see past markets?">
    Yes. Your complete market history — past participation, payments, and
    messages — is available in your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="message" title="Can I message hosts?">
    Yes. Use DMs from the market details page or the Messages tab in the app.
    Markets also have a group chat channel in the mobile app for open
    discussion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Can I also become a market host?">
    Yes. Your Seen Markets account can be both a vendor and a host. Create an
    organization from your account menu and switch between contexts. See the
    [become a host](/how-to/become-a-host) guide for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
