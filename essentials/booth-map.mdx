## Overview

The Booth Map Designer helps you create professional, interactive layouts of your market space. Easily design your venue layout, manage booth assignments, and provide clear navigation for vendors and shoppers.

## Creating Your Market Layout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Layout">
    Click "Create New Layout" in the Booth Map section of your market dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Dimensions">
    Define your market space: - Overall market dimensions - Aisle widths -
    Entry/exit points
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Booth Spaces">
    Create booth spaces with: - Custom dimensions - Unique identifiers (e.g.,
    "A1", "B2") - Special attributes (power access, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Features">
    Add market features: - Restrooms - Information booth - Food court areas -
    Emergency exits
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Booth Types and Customization

### Standard Booth Options

- Single booth (10' x 10')
- Double booth (10' x 20')
- Corner booth
- End cap booth
- Food truck space
- Custom dimensions

### Booth Attributes

- Power access
- Water access
- Shade coverage
- Surface type
- Special requirements

## Assigning Vendors

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Assignment Tool">
    Open the vendor assignment interface from your booth map
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Booth">Click on an available booth space</Step>
  <Step title="Choose Vendor">
    Select from your approved vendor list or create a new vendor
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Details">
    Verify booth requirements match vendor needs
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Pro Tip: Use the bulk assignment feature for recurring markets with consistent
  vendor placements.
</Note>
