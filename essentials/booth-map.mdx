## Overview

The Booth Map Designer helps you create professional, interactive layouts of your market space. Design your venue layout, manage booth assignments, and provide clear navigation for vendors and shoppers.

## Accessing the Designer

Access the Booth Map Designer directly from your market's management page:

  <img
    src="/images/market-management-page.png"
    alt="Market Management Dashboard"
  />
</Frame>

## Creating Your Layout

When you first open the designer, you'll see an empty canvas ready for your market layout:

<Frame caption="Empty Booth Map Designer">
  <img src="/images/empty-booth-map.png" alt="Empty Booth Map Designer" />
</Frame>

### Import Existing Layout

If you have an existing market layout you'd like to use:

- Click the "Import Layout" button
- Select the market you want to import the layout from
- The layout will be copied to your current market

### Adding Landmarks

Use landmarks to mark important locations in your market:

1. Click the "Add Landmark" button
2. Choose from preset options:
   - Entrance
   - Exit
   - Bathrooms
   - Doors
   - Power outlets
   - Custom (add your own label)
3. Place the landmark on your map

### Adding Booths

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Booths">
    Click the "Add Booths" button to open the booth creation dialog:
    <Frame caption="Add Booths Dialog">
      <img src="/images/add-booths.png" alt="Add Booths Dialog" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Specify Details">
    Enter: - Booth width - Booth height - Number of booths to create
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Booths">
    - Booths will appear in the map area - Drag and drop them to desired
    locations - Arrange them to match your venue layout
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Booths

Click on any booth to access management options:

<Frame caption="Booth Management Options">
  <img src="/images/booth-assign-vendor.png" alt="Booth Management Options" />
</Frame>

Available actions:

- Assign vendor to booth
- Rotate booth orientation
- Remove booth from map

<Note>
  Assigned booths will appear green in the designer to help you track which
  spaces are taken. This color coding is only visible in the designer view, not
  in the public Digital Market Guide.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="ruler" title="Layout Planning">
    - Start with landmarks to establish key reference points - Group similar
    booth sizes together - Leave adequate space for foot traffic - Consider
    power outlet access when placing booths
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="arrows-to-dot" title="Booth Organization">
    - Use consistent orientation within sections - Create clear aisles and
    pathways - Place similar vendors near each other - Ensure easy access to
    amenities
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="pen-to-square" title="Design Tips">
    - Label landmarks clearly - Verify booth dimensions match vendor needs -
    Double-check vendor assignments - Review layout from shopper perspective
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
