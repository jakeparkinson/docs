## Overview

Your vendor page is a public profile for your business on Seen Markets. It's where shoppers go to learn about you, see your upcoming events, browse your products, and sign up to hear from you. Every vendor gets one automatically — you just need to fill it in.

Your vendor page URL looks like `seenmarkets.com/vendor/your-name` (with a paid plan) or `seenmarkets.com/vendor/your-id` (free).

## Accessing your vendor page

Go to [seenmarkets.com/v/profile](https://seenmarkets.com/v/profile) to manage your vendor page. You can also get there from the sidebar by clicking **Vendor Page**.

At the top of the page, use **View your page** to see what shoppers see.

## Setting up your page

The profile editor is organized into sections. Use the sticky navigation bar to jump between them.

### Basics

- **Logo** — your business logo. Appears on your vendor page, booth maps, and market listings.
- **Tagline** — a short line that appears under your name (e.g., "Handmade soy candles for every season").
- **Description** — tell shoppers what you sell and what makes your business unique.

### Gallery

Upload photos of your products, booth setup, and anything else you want shoppers to see. Drag to reorder. Images must be under 4 MB each.

Your gallery appears as a scrollable section on your vendor page. Shoppers can tap any photo to view it full-screen.

### Business info

- **Legal business name** and **DBA** (doing business as).
- **Phone number** and **website**.
- **Address** — used for location context on your page. Your full street address is not shown publicly.

### Social links

Add your accounts for Instagram, TikTok, X, Facebook, and YouTube. These appear as icons on your vendor page so shoppers can follow you.

### Payment methods

Add your Venmo, Cash App, and PayPal usernames. Shoppers see these on your page under a **Pay** section so they can send you money directly at events.

## Customizing your brand

<Note>
  Custom branding requires the Essentials plan ($3.99/mo) or higher.
</Note>

Click **My Brand** at the top of the profile editor to open branding settings.

### Custom URL

Choose a custom slug for your vendor page URL: `seenmarkets.com/vendor/yourname`. Use this on business cards, social bios, and signage. You can check availability before saving.

### Brand colors

Set a **brand color** and **accent color**. These are applied to your vendor page — buttons, links, and the Notify Me form all use your brand colors.

### Banner image

Upload a banner image that appears at the top of your vendor page. Use a wide, high-quality photo of your booth, products, or branding.

## Booth Magnet QR code

<Note>
  The QR code requires the Essentials plan ($3.99/mo) or higher.
</Note>

Your vendor page has a built-in QR code that links directly to it. Display this at your booth so shoppers can scan it, see your page, and sign up for your email list on the spot.

To download your QR code:

1. Go to your profile editor and click the **QR code** button at the top.
2. Choose a size: 512px (social media), 1024px (print), or 2048px (large format).
3. Download and print it.

When a shopper scans the code, they land on your vendor page where they can browse your info and subscribe via the **Notify Me** form.

<Note>
  Every email captured through your QR code or Notify Me form goes straight to your [email list](/essentials/vendor-email-list).
</Note>

## Event schedule

Your vendor page shows an **Upcoming Events** section with all the markets you're attending.

### Seen Markets events

Any market where you're approved as a vendor automatically appears on your event schedule. You don't need to add these manually.

### Outside events

<Note>
  Outside events require the Growth plan ($4.99/mo) or higher.
</Note>

Add events that aren't on Seen Markets — craft fairs, pop-ups, festivals, or any other event you're attending. Each outside event includes a name, date, location, and optional link.

This gives shoppers a complete picture of where to find you, not just Seen Markets events.

### Invite a host

Know a market host who isn't on Seen Markets yet? Use the **Invite a Host** feature from your event schedule section to send them a referral email. You can also copy a personal follow-up script to send yourself.

## Shop links

<Note>
  Shop links require the Growth plan ($4.99/mo) or higher.
</Note>

Add products to your vendor page with an image, title, description, price, and a buy link to your online store (Shopify, Etsy, Square, or any URL). Shoppers who visit your booth can buy from you later with one tap.

Drag to reorder products. Each product appears as a card in the **Shop** section of your vendor page.

## Notify Me

Your vendor page includes a **Get my event schedule** button. When a shopper taps it, a form asks for their name and email, with an optional checkbox to opt into marketing updates.

After subscribing, the shopper sees a confirmation: "We'll let you know when [your name] is at an upcoming event."

Contacts collected through this form appear in your [email list](/essentials/vendor-email-list). If the shopper opts into marketing, they're also eligible for [Auto-Emails](/essentials/vendor-auto-emails).

## Sharing your page

Click **Share** at the top of the profile editor to get sharing options:

- **Copy link** — copy your vendor page URL.
- **Social sharing** — share directly to social platforms.
- **QR code** — download your QR code for print.

Add your vendor page link to your Instagram bio, email signature, business cards, and anywhere else shoppers can find you.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="eye" title="Can shoppers see my page if I'm on the free plan?">
    Yes. Every vendor has a public page. Free pages use a default URL with your
    vendor ID and don't include custom branding, but all the basics are there.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="image" title="What size should my images be?">
    Images must be under 4 MB. For best results, use landscape-oriented photos
    at least 1200px wide. The banner image should be wide and short — think
    social media cover photo dimensions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="link" title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Not currently. Your vendor page lives at `seenmarkets.com/vendor/yourname`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bell" title="What happens when someone uses Notify Me?">
    Their name and email are added to your [email list](/essentials/vendor-email-list).
    If they opted into marketing, they'll receive your
    [Auto-Emails](/essentials/vendor-auto-emails) (Maximize plan).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
