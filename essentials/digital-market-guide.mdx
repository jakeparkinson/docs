## Setting Up Your Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Guide Settings">
    From your market dashboard, navigate to the Digital Market Guide section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Content">
    Add your market description, hours, and any special instructions for
    visitors
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Display Options">
    Choose which features to display: - Booth map visibility - Vendor directory
    layout - Giveaway promotions - Email collection form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish">
    Review how your guide looks on mobile devices and publish when ready
  </Step>
</Steps>

## QR Code Distribution

### Downloading Your QR Code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate QR Code">
    Click "Generate QR Code" in the Digital Market Guide settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Format">
    Select your preferred format: - High-resolution PNG for print - SVG for
    scalable graphics - PDF with instructions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">Save the QR code files to your device</Step>
</Steps>

### Display Recommendations

Place QR codes in these strategic locations:

- Market entrances
- Information booths
- Vendor stalls
- Promotional materials
- Social media posts
