## Overview

The Digital Market Guide transforms your market into an interactive digital experience, helping shoppers discover vendors and navigate your market with ease. Every market on Seen Markets automatically includes a Digital Market Guide - no setup required.

## Accessing Your Guide

Your Digital Market Guide is automatically created when you create your market. To access it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Market Management">
    From your dashboard, open your market's management page
    <Frame caption="Market Management Page - Click 'View Digital Market Guide' to preview and get your QR code">
      <img
        src="/images/market-management-page.png"
        alt="Market Management Dashboard"
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View and Share">
    Click the "View Digital Market Guide" button to: - Preview your guide
    exactly as shoppers will see it - Download your market's unique QR code -
    Get a shareable link for digital distribution
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your Digital Market Guide updates automatically as you make changes to your
  market, such as updating the booth map or adding giveaways.
</Note>

## QR Code Distribution

### Downloading Your QR Code

From the Digital Market Guide preview window, click the "Download QR Code" button to get your market's unique QR code.

<Frame caption="Digital Market Guide preview window with QR code download option">
  <img
    src="/images/digital-market-guide-modal.png"
    alt="Digital Market Guide Modal"
  />
</Frame>

You can then:

- Print it yourself
- Order professional signage through our print shop
- Share it digitally

### Display Recommendations

Place QR codes in these strategic locations:

- Market entrances
- Information booths
- Vendor stalls
- Promotional materials
- Social media posts

<Note>
  Pro Tip: Order weather-resistant QR code signage through our print shop for
  professional display options.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wifi" title="Does it work offline?">
    The Digital Market Guide requires an internet connection to access. However,
    once loaded, basic features will continue to work with limited connectivity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="mobile-screen" title="What devices are supported?">
    The guide works on any modern smartphone or tablet with a web browser. No
    app download required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="share" title="Can I share the guide without the QR code?">
    Yes, you can share the direct link to your Digital Market Guide through
    email, social media, or your market's website.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
