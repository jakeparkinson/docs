## Overview

Create exciting giveaways featuring prizes from your market vendors. Giveaways help drive foot traffic to vendor booths while building your market's social media following.

## Creating a Giveaway

Creating a giveaway is a simple three-step process:

### Step 1: Giveaway Details

<Frame caption="Giveaway Details Form">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-step-1.png" alt="Giveaway Step 1" />
</Frame>

Fill out basic information about your giveaway:

- Giveaway name
- Description
- Start and end dates
- Entry requirements

### Step 2: Add Prizes

<Frame caption="Prize Selection">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-step-2.png" alt="Giveaway Step 2" />
</Frame>

- Select prizes from participating vendors
- Add multiple prizes from different vendors
- Each vendor's Instagram account will be included for following requirements

<Note>
  You can create multiple giveaways for your market, each with different vendor
  prizes and timeframes.
</Note>

### Step 3: Legal Requirements

Review and customize:

- Standard legal terms
- Optional privacy policy
- Entry rules and conditions

## Managing Active Giveaways

Once created, you can manage your giveaway:

<Frame caption="Active Giveaway Management">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-created.png" alt="Active Giveaway" />
</Frame>

Features include:

- View all entries
- Select random winner
- Send winner notifications
- Track prize claims

### Selecting and Notifying Winners

1. Click "Pick Winner" to randomly select an entrant
2. Click "Notify Winner" to send prize claim instructions
3. Enter specific instructions for claiming the prize
4. Winner receives email with claim details

### Sharing Your Giveaway

Access the giveaway URL through the three-dot menu:

<Frame caption="Giveaway Sharing Options">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-url.png" alt="Giveaway URL Access" />
</Frame>

Share your giveaway:

- Through the Digital Market Guide
- Direct URL sharing
- Social media posts
- Market communications

## How Shoppers Enter

Shoppers enter giveaways by completing a form that collects:

<Frame caption="Giveaway Entry Form">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-entry-filled-out.png" alt="Giveaway Entry Form" />
</Frame>

Required Information:

- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Instagram Username

Entry Requirements:

- Must follow participating vendors on Instagram (if vendor provided account)
- Instagram username used to verify follows
- Optional opt-in for market and vendor marketing

### Post-Entry Experience

After entering, shoppers see:

<Frame caption="Post-Entry Success Page">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-post-entry.png" alt="Post-Entry Success" />
</Frame>

Features:

- List of participating vendors
- Interactive booth map links
- Instagram story sharing option
- Direct links to vendor booth locations

<Note>
  The post-entry experience helps drive foot traffic to vendor booths by showing
  exactly where participating vendors are located in your market.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="instagram" title="Why require Instagram follows?">
    Following vendors on Instagram helps build lasting connections between
    shoppers and vendors, extending beyond market day.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="gift" title="How are winners selected?">
    Winners are randomly selected using the "Pick Winner" button. The system
    ensures fair and unbiased selection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="hand" title="How do winners claim prizes?">
    Winners receive an email with specific claim instructions provided by the
    market host. Coordination happens between the market host and winner.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
