## Overview

Create giveaways featuring prizes from your market vendors. Giveaways drive foot traffic to vendor booths, build your market's social media following, and collect shopper contact info for future marketing.

## Accessing Giveaways

Open your market's management page and click the **Giveaways** card.

## Creating a Giveaway

### Step 1: Giveaway Details

<Frame caption="Giveaway Details Form">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-step-1.png" alt="Giveaway Step 1" />
</Frame>

Fill out:

- **Giveaway name**
- **Description**
- **Start and end dates**
- **Entry requirements**

### Step 2: Add Prizes

<Frame caption="Prize Selection">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-step-2.png" alt="Giveaway Step 2" />
</Frame>

- Select prizes from participating vendors (if vendors indicated prize contributions on their application).
- Add multiple prizes from different vendors.
- Each prize can include the vendor's name and value.

<Note>
  You can create multiple giveaways per market, each with different vendor
  prizes and timeframes.
</Note>

### Step 3: Social Follow Requirements

Configure which social accounts shoppers must follow to enter. You can require follows for the **market** and/or **participating vendors** on:

- **Instagram**
- **TikTok**
- **X (Twitter)**
- **Facebook**
- **YouTube**

This is optional — you can run giveaways without social follow requirements.

### Step 4: Legal Requirements

Review and customize:

- Standard legal terms.
- Optional privacy policy.
- Entry rules and conditions.

## Managing Active Giveaways

Once created, manage your giveaway:

<Frame caption="Active Giveaway Management">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-created.png" alt="Active Giveaway" />
</Frame>

- **View all entries** — see who entered.
- **Pick Winner** — randomly select an entrant.
- **Notify Winner** — send an email with prize claim instructions.
- **Resend notification** — resend the winner email if needed.

## Sharing Your Giveaway

Access the giveaway URL through the three-dot menu:

<Frame caption="Giveaway Sharing Options">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-url.png" alt="Giveaway URL Access" />
</Frame>

Share your giveaway:

- Through the **Digital Market Guide** (shoppers see it in the Giveaway tab).
- **Direct URL** sharing.
- **QR code** per giveaway (print and display at vendor booths).
- Social media and market communications.

## How Shoppers Enter

Shoppers open the giveaway (from the Digital Market Guide, direct URL, or QR code) and fill out:

<Frame caption="Giveaway Entry Form">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-entry-filled-out.png" alt="Giveaway Entry Form" />
</Frame>

Required information:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Social media username(s) (if social follow requirements are configured)

Optional:

- Opt-in for market and vendor marketing emails.

### Post-Entry Experience

After entering:

<Frame caption="Post-Entry Success Page">
  <img src="/images/giveaway-post-entry.png" alt="Post-Entry Success" />
</Frame>

- List of participating vendors.
- Interactive booth map links (find the vendor's booth).
- Social media sharing option.

<Note>
  The post-entry experience drives foot traffic to vendor booths by showing
  exactly where participating vendors are located.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="trophy" title="Prize Strategy">
    - Include multiple vendors to increase engagement.
    - Coordinate prize details with vendors in advance.
    - Consider themed giveaway bundles.
    - Use the giveaway participation section on the application form to collect prize offers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Timing">
    - Run giveaways during market hours for maximum entries.
    - Plan winner selection during the market event.
    - Allow enough time for entries.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bullhorn" title="Promotion">
    - Print QR codes and display at vendor booths.
    - Share through all market channels.
    - Encourage vendors to promote on their social media.
    - Post reminders during the event.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="share-nodes" title="Why require social follows?">
    Following vendors on social media builds lasting connections between
    shoppers and vendors, extending beyond market day. But it's optional —
    you can run giveaways without social requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="gift" title="How are winners selected?">
    Winners are randomly selected using the **Pick Winner** button. The system
    ensures fair selection from all entries.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="hand" title="How do winners claim prizes?">
    Winners receive an email with claim instructions you provide. You
    coordinate prize delivery between the vendor and winner.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
