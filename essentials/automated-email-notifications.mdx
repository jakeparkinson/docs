## Overview

Seen Markets automatically sends email notifications to keep vendors, hosts, and shoppers informed. You can customize status update templates and control which notifications are sent from [seenmarkets.com/settings/email-preferences](https://seenmarkets.com/settings/email-preferences).

## Vendor Notifications

### Application Status Updates

Sent when you change a vendor's status:

- **Approved** — vendor is accepted.
- **Denied** — vendor is not accepted.
- **Waitlisted** — vendor is on the waiting list.
- **Cancelled** — vendor's participation is cancelled.

<Note>
  You can **customize the content** of each status email template. Go to
  Settings → Email Preferences & Templates to edit the approved, denied,
  waitlist, and cancelled email templates for your organization. Each template
  can have different messaging.
</Note>

### Welcome Email

Sent when a vendor submits their first application:

- Introduces Seen Markets.
- Explains the vendor portal.
- Provides login information and next steps.

### Payment Notifications

**Payment requests:** Sent when you request payment from a vendor — includes the amount, description, and a secure Stripe checkout link.

**Payment cancellations:** Sent when you cancel a payment request.

### Market Updates

Sent when you use **Send Update** to share announcements with vendors — schedule changes, weather alerts, setup instructions, reminders, etc.

### Vendor Cancellation

Sent to the host when a vendor cancels their participation in a market.

## Host Notifications

### New Application Alert

Sent when a vendor submits an application:

- Vendor business name.
- Link to review the application.
- Quick action buttons.

### Updated Application

Sent when a vendor updates their existing application.

### Market Creation Guide

Sent after you create a new market:

- Setup checklist and next steps.
- Important features to configure.

## Shopper Notifications

### Giveaway Winner

Sent to giveaway winners with:

- Prize details.
- Claim instructions from the host.
- Market and vendor information.

## Email Preferences

Control which automated emails are sent from **Settings → Email Preferences & Templates** at [seenmarkets.com/settings/email-preferences](https://seenmarkets.com/settings/email-preferences):

- Toggle individual notification types on or off.
- Customize the content of status update emails (approved, denied, waitlist, cancelled).
- Changes apply to all markets in your organization.

## Email Logs

View a history of all sent emails at **Settings → Email Logs** ([seenmarkets.com/settings/email-logs](https://seenmarkets.com/settings/email-logs)). See delivery status, recipient, and content for every email your organization has sent.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Can I customize these emails?">
    Yes. Status update emails (approved, denied, waitlist, cancelled) have
    customizable templates in Settings → Email Preferences. Market update
    content is written by you when you send it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock-rotate-left" title="How quickly are emails sent?">
    Emails are queued and sent within seconds of the triggering event.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="envelope-open" title="What if a vendor doesn't receive an email?">
    Check Email Logs to confirm delivery. Ask the vendor to check their spam
    folder and add Seen Markets to their safe senders. Contact support if issues
    persist.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
