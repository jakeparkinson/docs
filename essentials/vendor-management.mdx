## Overview

The Vendor Management system helps you track and manage all of your market vendors in one place. From application review to payment collection, you can handle all vendor-related tasks directly from the vendor management table.

### Adding Vendors

There are two ways to add vendors to your market:

1. **Through Application Form (Recommended)**

   - Vendors apply through your market's application form
   - Ensures all vendor information is up-to-date
   - Collects responses to your custom questions

2. **Import from Previous Markets**
   - Import vendors from other markets you've managed
   - Standard vendor information is transferred
   - Note: Custom question responses won't be imported

### Vendor Status Management

Each vendor row shows key information and status:

- **Status Options:**
  - Pending (default)
  - Approved
  - Denied
  - Waitlisted

<Note>
  Vendors automatically receive email notifications when you change their
  status.
</Note>

### Viewing Vendor Details

Expand any vendor row to see:

- Complete application form responses
- Payment history
- Booth assignments
- Giveaway prize contributions
- Transaction history

## Payment Management

### Requesting Payment

1. Click the "Request Payment" button for a vendor
2. Enter the requested amount
3. Specify the line item (e.g., "Booth Fee Collection")
4. Submit the request

<Note>
  Payment processing is handled through Stripe. You'll need to set up your
  Stripe account to process payments.
</Note>

### Payment Status Tracking

Monitor payment status directly in the vendor table:

- Pending: Payment has been requested
- Complete: Payment has been received
- Cancelled: Payment request was cancelled

### Managing Payment Requests

In the expanded vendor view, you can:

- Cancel pending payment requests
- Resend payment links
- View complete transaction history
- Track all payments for the market

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion
    icon="envelope"
    title="How are vendors notified of status changes?"
  >
    Vendors receive automatic email notifications whenever you change their
    status (approved, denied, or waitlisted).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="credit-card" title="How do vendors pay their fees?">
    Vendors receive an email with a payment link when you request payment. They
    can pay securely through Stripe.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="file-import" title="What happens when I import vendors?">
    Basic vendor information transfers over, but they'll need to complete any
    custom questions specific to the new market.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
