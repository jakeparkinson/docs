## Creating Your Application Form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Form Builder">
    Access the form builder from your market dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Basic Information">
    Include essential fields: - Business name - Contact information - Product
    category - Business description
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Requirements">
    Add market-specific fields: - Booth size preferences - Power/water needs -
    Insurance information - Required permits
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Documents">
    Request necessary files: - Product photos - Insurance certificates -
    Business licenses - Health permits
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Applications

### Review Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Applications">
    View submitted applications in your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Details">
    Evaluate vendor information: - Product fit - Required documentation - Space
    requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Decision">
    Choose to: - Approve - Waitlist - Deny - Request more information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Response">
    Automated notifications inform vendors of their status
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions
