## Overview

Send announcements and updates to all vendors in your market. Updates are delivered via email and appear in each vendor's portal and mobile app for future reference.

## Sending an Update

### From the Web

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your market">
    Go to [seenmarkets.com](https://seenmarkets.com) and open your market's management page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send Update">
    Find the **Send Update** option (or "Notifications" section on the market page).

    <Frame caption="Markets List Page - Click 'Send Update' to message all vendors">
      <img src="/images/send-update.png" alt="Markets List Page" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your update">
    Fill out the form:
    - **Title** — a clear subject line.
    - **Message** — the body of your announcement (supports rich text).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send Update**. Every vendor in the market receives an email, and the message is posted to their portal / app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From the Mobile App

Open a market in the app → tap **Send notification** → enter a title and message → send. Same result — email to all vendors, plus it appears in their app.

## What updates are good for

- Weather alerts and schedule changes
- Setup and teardown instructions
- Market reminders and deadlines
- Policy updates
- General announcements

## Direct Messaging and Market Chat

For conversations with vendors beyond broadcast updates, you have two more options:

### Direct Messages (DMs)

One-on-one private conversations with a specific vendor:

- **Web:** Open the vendor management table → click the message icon on a vendor row.
- **App:** Open a market → vendor list → tap a vendor to DM them. Or use the **Messages** tab.

DMs are per-market, so conversations stay organized by event.

### Market Chat (mobile app)

A **market-wide group chat** where you and your vendors can all communicate in one thread. Access it from the **Messages** tab in the mobile app. Useful for day-of coordination, open Q&A, or group announcements where you want vendor replies visible to everyone.

<Note>
  Use **Send Update** for formal announcements (email + portal). Use **DMs**
  for private conversations with one vendor. Use **Market Chat** for open
  group discussion.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="pen" title="Writing Tips">
    - Use clear, descriptive titles.
    - Keep messages concise and actionable.
    - Include all relevant details (dates, locations, requirements).
    - Specify any required actions from vendors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Timing">
    - Send updates during business hours when possible.
    - Give adequate notice for changes.
    - Avoid last-minute announcements unless urgent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Who receives updates?">
    All vendors currently in your market receive the update via email and in
    their portal/app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock-rotate-left" title="Can vendors see past updates?">
    Yes. All updates are preserved in the vendor portal and mobile app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="message" title="Can vendors reply?">

    app.
  </Accordion>
