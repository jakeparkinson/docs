## Overview

Send important announcements and updates to all vendors in your market. Updates are delivered via email and also appear in each vendor's portal for future reference.

## Sending an Update

### Step 1: Access Your Markets

Navigate to your markets list by clicking "Markets" in the main navigation.

<Frame caption="Markets List Page - Click 'Send Update' to message all vendors">
  <img src="/images/send-update.png" alt="Markets List Page" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Create Your Update

1. Click the "Send Update" button for the market you want to message
2. Fill out the update form:
   - Title: A clear subject for your update
   - Message: The body of your announcement

### Step 3: Send

Click "Send Update" to:

- Send an email to all vendors in your market
- Post the message to each vendor's portal

<Note>
  Updates are a great way to share important information like: - Weather alerts
  - Schedule changes - Setup instructions - Market reminders - Policy updates
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="pen" title="Writing Tips">
    - Use clear, descriptive titles - Keep messages concise - Include all
    relevant details - Specify any required actions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Timing">
    - Send updates during business hours - Allow adequate notice for changes -
    Consider vendor preparation time - Avoid last-minute announcements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="list-check" title="Content Guidelines">
    - Focus on one topic per update - Include deadlines if applicable - Provide
    contact information - Specify next steps if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Who receives updates?">
    All vendors currently participating in your market will receive the update
    via email and in their vendor portal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="clock-rotate-left" title="Can vendors see past updates?">
    Yes, all updates are preserved in the vendor portal for future reference.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="reply" title="How can vendors respond to updates?">
    While vendors cannot reply directly in the app, they can reach out to you
    using your market host contact information.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
