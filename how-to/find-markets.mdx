## Three ways to find markets

### 1. Find a Local Market (web)

Visit [seenmarkets.com/find-a-local-market](https://seenmarkets.com/find-a-local-market) — a public directory of published markets on Seen Markets.

- **Browse by state** — see all markets in a specific state.
- **Search** — type a market name or location to find it.
- **Near me** — use your location to find nearby markets.

Each result shows the market name, dates, location, and a link to view the market page or apply.

### 2. Explore tab (mobile app)

In the Seen Markets app, the **Explore** tab shows:

- **Markets from hosts you've worked with** — if you've applied to a host's event before, their other upcoming events appear here. Tap "Join" to apply.
- **"Find events near you"** — opens a search where you can look up markets by name, location, state, or use your current location.

### 3. Direct links from hosts

Many hosts share their links directly:

- **Application form link** — the host sends you a direct URL to apply (e.g. `seenmarkets.com/markets/.../apply`). This is the most common way.
- **Schedule page** — a host's public schedule showing all their upcoming events. You can apply to any of them.
- **Market page** — a public marketing page for a specific event with a link to apply.

## After you find a market

Tap **Apply** (or open the application link) to fill out the vendor application form. See [How to apply to a market](/how-to/apply-to-market) for the full step-by-step.

## Tips

- Check the Explore tab regularly — new events from hosts you know appear automatically.
- Use "Find events near you" if you're looking for markets in a new area.
- Follow hosts on social media — they often post application links there.
