## Three things you can share

Every market has three shareable links. Each serves a different purpose.

### 1. Application form

**Who it's for:** Potential vendors.

**What it is:** The direct link vendors use to apply to your event.

**URL format:** `https://seenmarkets.com/markets/[your-market-id]/apply`

**Where to get it:**
- **Web:** Market management page → Application Form section → copy the link.
- **App:** Open the market → **Share** → **Application Form**.

Share this link via email, social media, your website, or anywhere vendors will see it.

### 2. Market page

**Who it's for:** Everyone — vendors, shoppers, sponsors.

**What it is:** A public marketing page with your market name, dates, location, vendor list, and a link to the application form.

**Where to get it:**
- **Web:** Market management page → copy the market page link.
- **App:** Open the market → **Share** → **Market Page**.

Post this on social media, your website, or send to anyone who wants to learn about your event.

### 3. Digital Market Guide + QR code

**Who it's for:** Shoppers at the market.

**What it is:** An interactive page with your booth map, vendor directory, giveaways, and community signup.

**URL format:** `https://seenmarkets.com/hub/[your-market-id]`

**Where to get it:**
- **Web:** Market management page → Digital Market Guide card → **Download QR Code** or copy the link.
- **App:** Open the market → **Share** → **Digital Market Guide**. Download or share the QR code.

**How to use the QR code:**
- Print it on signage and display at market entrances.
- Order professional signs through the [Print Shop](/services/print-shop).
- Include in flyers, social posts, or emails before the event.
- Display at vendor booths.

## Your schedule page

Your organization also has a **public schedule page** showing all upcoming events.

**Where to get it:** In the mobile app, go to the Schedule tab and copy the schedule page URL. On the web, the URL is: `https://seenmarkets.com/market-schedule/[your-team-id]/[your-team-name]`

Share this so vendors and shoppers can see your full calendar and apply to any event.

## Quick reference

| What to share | Who it's for | Where to get it |
|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Application form link | Vendors | Share → Application Form |
| Market page | Everyone | Share → Market Page |
| Digital Market Guide / QR code | Shoppers | Share → Digital Market Guide |
| Schedule page | Vendors + shoppers | Schedule tab in app or web |
