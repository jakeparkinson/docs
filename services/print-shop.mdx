## Overview

The Print Shop helps market hosts and vendors create professional marketing materials. From labels to banners, business cards to packaging, we offer a variety of products with customizable templates or the option to use your own designs.

## Accessing the Print Shop

You can access the Print Shop in two ways:

1. Visit [seenmarkets.com/print](https://seenmarkets.com/print)
2. In the app: Navigate to Marketing -> Order Signs, Banners, & Prints

## Design Process

### Step 1: Choose Your Product

Browse our product categories to find what you need:

- Select from various product types
- Review size options
- Check material choices
- Compare pricing

### Step 2: Select a Design

<Frame caption="Browse Design Templates">
  <img
    src="/images/print-shop-browse-design.png"
    alt="Browse Design Templates"
  />
</Frame>

Choose from three options:

1. **Use a Template** (Recommended)

   - Professional pre-made designs
   - Easy to customize
   - Market-tested layouts

2. **Upload Your Design**

   - Use your own artwork
   - Import existing materials
   - Complete creative control

3. **Start Blank**
   - Create from scratch
   - Full design freedom
   - No design constraints

### Step 3: Customize Your Design

<Frame caption="Design Editor">
  <img src="/images/print-shop-edit.png" alt="Design Editor" />
</Frame>

Our editor lets you:

- Add and edit text
- Upload images
- Insert QR codes
- Adjust colors
- Change layouts
- Modify elements

<Note>
  For market hosts: You can easily add your Digital Market Guide QR code to any
  design.
</Note>

### Step 4: Print & Ship

Once your design is complete:

1. Click "Print & Ship"
2. Review your design
3. Select quantity
4. Enter shipping details
5. Complete your order

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="pen-ruler" title="Design Tips">
    - Use high-resolution images - Keep text readable - Include contact
    information - Maintain consistent branding
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="qrcode" title="QR Code Placement">
    - Ensure adequate size - Maintain quiet zone around code - Test scan before
    ordering - Include call-to-action text
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="box" title="Ordering Tips">
    - Order extras for backup - Allow time for shipping - Consider weather
    durability - Check measurements twice
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
